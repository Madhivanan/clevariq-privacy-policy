Privacy Policy for CleverIQ

Last Updated: December 10, 2025

CleverIQ ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users ("you" or "your"), especially children. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • We do not ask for names, email addresses, phone numbers, or locations.
  • We do not require account registration.
  • All game progress (profiles, scores, level data) is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, this data is lost.

2. Advertising (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements to support the app. We have configured AdMob to be Child-Directed (COPPA Compliant).

  • Non-Personalized Ads: We only request non-personalized ads. This means ads are based on the content of the app, not on a user's past behavior or personal data.
  • Restricted Categories: We have blocked sensitive categories and ads not suitable for children (e.g., gambling, alcohol, dating).
  • Ad Tracking: We do not use Advertising IDs for interest-based advertising / remarketing.

For more information on Google AdMob's privacy practices, please visit: Google Privacy Policy.
